About.

I'm a full-stack developer in Kathmandu. I like taking an idea the whole way — from the blank file to something real people can log into and use.

Most of what I build is full-stack web: Python and Flask on the back, clean responsive UI on the front, real databases (PostgreSQL, SQLite, Supabase), auth, payments, and chat. Both of my shipped products solve actual Nepali problems — connecting brands with creators, and households with reliable technicians. I care about shipping things that work in the wild, not things that only look good in a demo.

I own the whole stack myself: data models, routes, auth, deployment on Render, and the interface on top. When something breaks at 2am, it's my problem to fix — and I like it that way. Always learning what comes next.
